Chess with voice

Here is a chess board. You can only make legal chess moves. When you hover over a piece, the legal moves will be highlighted.

How to use Alan Voice Assistant

Click the blue button in the lower right to activate Alan.

Try saying something like "Move pawn to d4," "e4," or "Knight f3."

Alan will move the piece for you. You may use the phonetic alphabet (alpha, bravo, charlie, ...) for clarity.

This website is made with the chess.js library, the chessboard.js library, and Alan AI. The source code is available on GitHub.
